The linear transformation that transforms every vector to itself is called the **identity tensor**. 
$$
\mathbf{I}\mathbf{a}=\mathbf{a}
$$

The components are 
$$
\mathbf{I}_{ij} = \mathbf{e}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{I}\mathbf{e}_{j} = \mathbf{e}_{i}\cdot \mathbf{e}_{j}= \delta_{ij}
$$

With 
$$
[\mathbf{I}] = \begin{bmatrix}
1 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & 1 & 0 \\
0 & 0 & 1 
\end{bmatrix}
$$
