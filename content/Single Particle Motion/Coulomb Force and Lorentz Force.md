Charged particles at rest are the source of the electrostatic field, $\mathbf{E}$, which is the origin of the **Coulomb force**
$$
 \mathbf{F}_{C} = q\mathbf{E}
$$
they feel in the combined electrostatic field of all other particles. 

Charged particles moving with a velocity $\mathbf{v}$, are current elements generating a magnetic field, $\mathbf{B}$, which is the origin of the **Lorentz Force**
$$
\mathbf{F}_{L} = q(\mathbf{v}\times \mathbf{B})
$$
So, the motion of charged particles is strongly influenced by the presence of the electromagnetic field, while at the same time it is also the source of the fields.
