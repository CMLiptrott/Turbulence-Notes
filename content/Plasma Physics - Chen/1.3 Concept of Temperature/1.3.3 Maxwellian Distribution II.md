Starting with the [[1.3.1 Maxwellian Distribution|one-dimensional Maxwellian distribution]], 
$$
 f(u) = A\exp\left( -\frac{1}{2} \mu^2 /KT\right),
$$
The constant $A$ is related to the [[1.3.2 Number Density|density]] $n$, by 
 $$
A = n \left( \frac{m}{2\pi KT} \right)^{1/2}
$$
The width of the distribution is characterised by the constant $T$, which we call the [[1.3.4 Temperature]|temperature]].
