The density $n$, or number of particles per $m^3$, is given by 
<p style="text-transform: uppercase; font-size: 0.8em; font-weight: bold; margin-bottom: 0;text-align: center;">Number Density</p>
$$
n = \int_{\infty}^{\infty}f(u)du
$$
Here $f(u)$ is the [[Maxwellian Distribution|one-dimensional Maxwellian distribution]].
